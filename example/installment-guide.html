Get the electronics you need today and pay over time. Our hassle-free installment plans are designed to make premium technology accessible to everyone.

2

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any interest or markup on installments? +
We offer 0% markup on most of our products. Some premium items may have a minimal processing fee which is disclosed upfront before purchase.
What happens if I miss a payment? +
We understand life happens. Contact us before the due date and we'll work with you to reschedule. Repeated missed payments may incur a late fee.
Can I pay off my installment early? +
Absolutely! You can pay off your remaining balance at any time without any early payment penalties.
Do I get warranty on installment products? +
Yes! All products come with full official warranty regardless of whether you pay in full or via installment.
